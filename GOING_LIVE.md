# GraceConnect Going Live

GraceConnect is a flexible community organisation template that works for churches, sports clubs, RSL clubs, surf clubs, rotary clubs, community centres, and not-for-profits.

## Recommended live integrations
- **alphinium-payments** for secure donations, recurring giving, tax receipts, and fund tracking.
- **alphinium-booking** for event registration, capacity limits, and volunteer signups.
- **ChatInstance** for visitor engagement, FAQs, and lead capture.

## Suggested launch checklist
1. Replace demo contact details and team profiles.
2. Connect alphinium-payments and configure donation funds and receipts.
3. Connect alphinium-booking to real event registrations.
4. Update Grace chat prompts for your organisation.
5. Add your branding, policies, and social links.
